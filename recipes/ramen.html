Ramen

Description

Spend a little time in prison and you'll learn all about the value of ramen noodles. Didn't think you could accidentally become a prostitute for soup? Surprise!!!

But hey, you might as well enjoy the ramen. It's not like you can just give it back at this point.

Ingredients

  • Package of ramen noodles
  • Water (Preferably hot and poured through a crack along the side of your cell door)
  1. Remove the ramen noodles from their packaging
  2. Carefully crush the noodles and powdered chicken together with a hammer
  3. Place large mouthfulls of crushed up noodles in your mouth
  4. Pour hot water into your mouth and gurgle
